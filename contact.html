Frequently Asked Questions
How do I track my order?
After placing your order, you'll receive a link to our live tracking page via SMS and email. You can see your rider's real-time location and estimated arrival time.
What if my food arrives cold or damaged?
We guarantee the quality of every delivery. If your order arrives cold or damaged, contact us within 30 minutes and we'll offer a full refund or replacement — no questions asked.
How do I apply a promo code?
Enter your promo code at checkout in the "Apply Code" field. Valid codes will instantly reduce your order total. Codes cannot be stacked or applied after payment.
Can I cancel or modify my order?
Orders can be cancelled within 5 minutes of placing them. After that, the restaurant may have started preparing your food. Use the live tracking page or contact us immediately.
How do I become a restaurant partner?
We love welcoming new restaurant partners! Fill out the contact form and select "Partner With Us" as your topic — our partnerships team will reach out within 24 hours.
